# American Express Platinum Card

**Annual Fee:** $895 (increased from $695 as of September 2025)
**Reset Type:** Calendar Year (January 1) for most credits; some quarterly

## Lounge Access

- **Centurion Lounges**: Unlimited access at 30+ locations
- **Delta Sky Club**: 10 complimentary visits per year when flying eligible Delta flights
- **Priority Pass Select**: Unlimited access to 1,300+ lounges worldwide
- **Escape Lounges**: Access included
- **Plaza Premium Lounges**: Access included

**Upcoming Lounges (2025-2026):**

## Points Earning

- **5X** on flights and prepaid hotels via Amex Travel (up to $500K annually)
- **1X** on all other purchases

## Travel Benefits

- Complimentary Marriott Bonvoy Gold Elite status
- Complimentary Hilton Honors Gold status
- Car rental loss and damage insurance
- Trip delay/cancellation insurance
- Baggage insurance
- No foreign transaction fees
