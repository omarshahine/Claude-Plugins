# Delta SkyMiles Reserve American Express Card

**Annual Fee:** $650
**Reset Type:** Mixed - some Calendar Year, some Medallion Year, some Account Anniversary

## Statement Credits

### Monthly Credits (Calendar Year)

| Credit | Amount | Frequency | Reset | Notes |
|--------|--------|-----------|-------|-------|
| **Resy Credit** | $240 | Monthly ($20) | 1st of month | Eligible US Resy restaurant purchases. Enrollment required |
| **Rideshare Credit** | $120 | Monthly ($10) | 1st of month | Select rideshare providers. Enrollment required |
| **Uber One Credit** | ~$120 | Monthly ($9.99) | 1st of month | ⚠️ **LIMITED-TIME PROMO** - Must enroll by June 25, 2026! Not a standing benefit. |

### Annual Credits

| Credit | Amount | Frequency | Reset | Notes |
|--------|--------|-----------|-------|-------|
| **Delta Stays Credit** | $200 | Annual | Account anniversary | Prepaid hotels/vacation rentals via delta.com/stays |
| **Companion Certificate** | Value varies | Annual | Account renewal | First, Comfort+, or Main cabin domestic/Caribbean/Central America. Taxes $22-$250. Not valid for Basic Economy |

### Multi-Year Credits

| Credit | Amount | Frequency | Reset |
|--------|--------|-----------|-------|
| **Global Entry/TSA PreCheck** | $120 (GE) / $85 (TSA) | Every 4-4.5 years | Application date |

## Lounge Access

### Delta Sky Club Access

| Level | Visits | How to Unlock |
|-------|--------|---------------|
| **Standard** | 15 visits per Medallion Year (resets Feb 1) | Included with card |
| **Unlimited** | Unlimited | Spend $75,000+ on card in calendar year |
| **Guest Passes** | 4 per calendar year | Included with card |

- Access when flying Delta (same-day boarding pass required)
- Complimentary access to Centurion Lounges when flying Delta
- Escape Lounge access when flying Delta

## Status Benefits

### Medallion Qualification

| Benefit | Value | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| **MQD Headstart** | $2,500 MQDs | Automatic each Medallion Qualification Year |
| **MQD Boost** | $1 MQD per $10 spent | Earn MQDs from card spending toward next year's status |

## Points Earning

- **3X** on eligible Delta purchases
- **1X** on all other purchases

## Travel Benefits

- **TakeOff 15**: 15% off award travel booked on delta.com or Fly Delta app (not partner flights)
- **First checked bag free** on Delta flights
- **Zone 5 Priority Boarding** on Delta flights
- **20% back** on Delta in-flight purchases (statement credit)
- No foreign transaction fees
