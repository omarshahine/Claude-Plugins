# Bank of America Alaska Airlines Atmos Rewards Cards

There are two consumer cards: **Ascent** (mid-tier) and **Summit** (premium). This document covers both.

---

## Atmos Rewards Summit Visa Infinite Card

**Annual Fee:** $395
**Reset Type:** Account Anniversary

### Statement Credits & Annual Benefits

| Benefit | Value | Frequency | Reset | Notes |
|---------|-------|-----------|-------|-------|
| **Alaska Lounge Passes** | 8 passes (2/quarter) | Quarterly | Each quarter | **Use-it-or-lose-it** - 2 passes per quarter, do NOT roll over. ~$130/quarter subjective value. Includes 2 children per pass. |
| **Wi-Fi Passes** | 8 passes | Annual | Account anniversary | Free Wi-Fi passes for Alaska flights |
| **Global Companion Award** | 25,000 points | Annual | Account anniversary | Points toward a companion fare |
| **Status Points** | 10,000 | Annual | Account anniversary | Starting with first anniversary |
| **Free Checked Bag** | ~$35-70/trip | Per flight | N/A | For cardholder + up to 6 guests on same reservation (when paid with card) |
| **Priority Boarding** | N/A | Per flight | N/A | For cardholder + up to 6 guests on same reservation |
| **Companion Fare** | $99 + taxes ($23-$250) | Annual | Account anniversary | After spending $6,000 on purchases. Valid on Alaska/Hawaiian flights |

### Special 2026 Benefits

- **Premium Bar Experience**: Through 2026, Summit cardholders get access to premium bar experience at Alaska Lounges (elevated wines, signature cocktails) when showing Summit Card

### Points Earning

- **3X** on Alaska Airlines and Hawaiian Airlines purchases
- **3X** on eligible dining purchases
- **3X** on foreign transaction purchases
- **1X** on all other purchases

### Bank of America Relationship Bonus

- **10% bonus** on all points earned from card spending if you have a Bank of America checking or savings account

## Atmos Rewards Ascent Visa Signature Card

**Annual Fee:** $95
**Reset Type:** Account Anniversary

### Statement Credits & Annual Benefits

| Benefit | Value | Frequency | Reset | Notes |
|---------|-------|-----------|-------|-------|
| **Companion Fare** | $99 + taxes ($23-$250) | Annual | Account anniversary | After spending $6,000 on purchases in previous anniversary year |

| **Priority Boarding** | N/A | Per flight | N/A | For cardholder + up to 6 guests |

### Bank of America Relationship Bonus

- **10% bonus** on all points earned from card spending if you have a Bank of America checking or savings account

## Common Travel Benefits (Both Cards)

- No foreign transaction fees
- Starlink Wi-Fi free for Atmos Rewards members (beginning 2026)
- oneworld alliance benefits (Hawaiian joining in 2026)

## New Earning Options (Later 2026)

Points and status points can be earned in three ways when flying:
1. **Distance traveled**: 1 point per mile flown
2. **Price paid**: 5 points per $1 spent on flights
3. **Segments flown**: 500 points per flight segment

## Upcoming 2026 Destinations

Alaska Airlines new European routes (Spring 2026):
- Rome
- London
- Reykjavik, Iceland
