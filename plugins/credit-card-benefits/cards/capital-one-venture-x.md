# Capital One Venture X Card

**Annual Fee:** $395
**Reset Type:** Account Anniversary for travel credit/bonus miles; Calendar Year for spending thresholds

## Statement Credits

### Anniversary-Based Credits

| Credit | Amount | Frequency | Reset | Notes |
|--------|--------|-----------|-------|-------|
| **Annual Travel Credit** | $300 | Annual | Account anniversary | Bookings through Capital One Travel portal only |
| **Anniversary Bonus Miles** | 10,000 miles | Annual | Account anniversary | Worth ~$100-185 toward travel |

### Multi-Year Credits

| Credit | Amount | Frequency | Reset |
|--------|--------|-----------|-------|
| **Global Entry/TSA PreCheck** | $120 (GE) / $85 (TSA) | Every 4 years | Application date |

## Lounge Access

- **Capital One Lounges**: Complimentary access at DFW, DEN, IAD, and coming soon to more locations
- **Capital One Landings**: Access at select airports
- **Priority Pass**: Unlimited access to 1,300+ lounges worldwide
- **Plaza Premium Lounges**: Access included

### Important 2026 Changes

**Effective February 1, 2026:**
- **Authorized User Access**: $125 annual fee per additional cardholder for lounge access
- **Guest Policy**: No more free guests at Capital One Lounges/Landings
  - $45 per adult guest
  - $25 per child guest (ages 2-17)
  - **Exception**: Free guest access restored if you spend $75,000+ on card in calendar year

**Through January 31, 2026:**
- Additional cardholders get free lounge access
- 2 guests at Capital One Lounges
- 1 guest at Capital One Landings

## Points Earning

- **10X** on hotels and rental cars via Capital One Travel
- **5X** on flights and vacation rentals via Capital One Travel
- **2X** on all other purchases

## Travel Benefits

- No foreign transaction fees
- Primary car rental coverage (domestic)
- Trip delay/cancellation insurance
- Lost luggage reimbursement
- Travel accident insurance
- Complimentary Hertz President's Circle status

## Total Potential Annual Value: ~$485+

The $300 travel credit + 10,000 bonus miles ($100-185 value) more than offsets the $395 annual fee.
